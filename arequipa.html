Arequipa, Peru - City Information and Places to Visit

About Arequipa

Welcome to Arequipa, the charming colonial city located in the Andes mountains of Peru. Known as the 'White City' due to its beautiful white volcanic stone architecture, Arequipa offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Explore its historic sites, indulge in local cuisine, and experience the warmth of its people.

Popular Attractions in Arequipa

Plaza de Armas

Visit the central square of Arequipa, surrounded by historic buildings, gardens, and a stunning cathedral. It's the heart of the city's social and cultural life.

Monasterio de Santa Catalina

Explore this 16th-century convent, known for its colorful walls, narrow streets, and peaceful atmosphere. It offers a glimpse into Arequipa's colonial past.

Mirador Yanahuara

Enjoy panoramic views of the city and the Misti Volcano from this picturesque viewpoint. It's a perfect spot for capturing memorable photos.

Colca Canyon

Embark on a day trip to Colca Canyon, one of the world's deepest canyons. Experience breathtaking landscapes, spot condors, and visit traditional villages.

Local Cuisine

Arequipa is renowned for its delicious culinary traditions. Don't miss trying local dishes such as rocoto relleno (stuffed peppers), adobo (marinated pork), and ceviche (fresh seafood marinated in citrus juices).

Plan Your Visit

Whether you're interested in history, outdoor adventures, or culinary delights, Arequipa has something to offer. Plan your visit to this enchanting city and immerse yourself in its unique atmosphere.
