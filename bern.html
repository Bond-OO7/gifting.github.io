Bern, Switzerland - City Information and Places to Visit

About Bern

Welcome to Bern, the picturesque capital city of Switzerland. Known for its well-preserved medieval architecture and charming old town, Bern offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Explore its cobbled streets, visit world-class museums, and experience the city's vibrant arts and culinary scenes.

Popular Landmarks and Attractions

Zytglogge (Clock Tower)

Admire the Zytglogge, Bern's iconic clock tower, located in the heart of the old town. Don't miss the hourly show where mechanical figures come to life.

Bern Munster (Bern Cathedral)

Visit the Bern Munster, Switzerland's tallest cathedral, and enjoy breathtaking views of the city from its tower. Explore the cathedral's stunning stained glass windows and architecture.

Bear Park

Discover Bear Park, a unique sanctuary for bears located along the Aare River. Observe these majestic animals in a natural environment designed to mimic their wild habitat.

Einstein House

Explore the former residence of Albert Einstein, the renowned physicist. The house is now a museum showcasing Einstein's life and achievements.

Cultural Experiences

Bern hosts a vibrant cultural scene, including theaters, galleries, and music venues. Attend a classical concert, enjoy contemporary art exhibitions, or experience the city's lively street festivals.
