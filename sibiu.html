About Sibiu

Welcome to Sibiu, one of Romania's most charming cities located in the heart of Transylvania. Known for its well-preserved medieval architecture, Sibiu offers a delightful blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Explore its cobblestone streets, visit historic landmarks, and immerse yourself in the city's warm atmosphere.
